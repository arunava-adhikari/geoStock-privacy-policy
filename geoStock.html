Privacy Policy for GeoStock

Effective Date: September 20, 2025
GeoStock ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Shopify app, GeoStock (the "App").

Information We Collect

When you install or use GeoStock, we may collect the following information:

  • Shopify Store Information: Store name, contact email, and other basic store details provided by Shopify.
  • Inventory Data: Product, stock, and location information required for the App to function.
  • Usage Data: Actions taken within the App, error logs, and technical details to improve performance.
  • Customer Data (if applicable): Limited customer location data if needed for displaying inventory availability.
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the App's features (e.g., inventory location management).
  • Improve and optimize the App's performance.
  • Communicate with you regarding support, updates, or issues.
  • Comply with legal or regulatory requirements.

Sharing of Information

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Shopify, as required to operate the App under Shopify's policies.
  • With service providers who assist in hosting, data storage, or analytics (under strict confidentiality agreements).
  • If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
We will never sell or rent your information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your store and inventory data only as long as your store is actively using the App. When you uninstall the App, we automatically delete store-related data within 30 days, unless legally required to keep it longer.

Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Request restrictions on processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting the new version on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
